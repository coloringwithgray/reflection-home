Reflections of (You)
Reflections of You is our inaugural fragrance at Coloring with Gray, designed to embody the art of collaboration. This "second skin" scent blends seamlessly with your natural chemistry, creating a unique and personal aroma. While it shines on its own, its true magic happens when it interacts with your skin, developing a fragrance that is distinctly yours. With Reflections of You, you become a co-creator, adding your unique essence to our art.
